About Peer Counselling

Peer counseling is an interactive relationship between individuals of similar age groups that fosters positive change and growth. It provides a platform for peers to engage in supportive discussions, address personal and academic challenges, and cultivate a sense of belonging. Peer counseling empowers individuals to take charge of their well-being, build resilience, and navigate life's challenges. By creating a nurturing environment, peer counseling enables participants to share experiences, seek advice, and develop meaningful relationships, ultimately leading to personal growth and positive change.
